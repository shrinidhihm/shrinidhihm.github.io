Aerodynamic Design of a Fowler Flap
for a Hybrid Laminar Flow Control Airfoil

Studienarbeit ⋅ TU Braunschweig ⋅ April 2025

Manual optimization using PyAeroSweep and SU2. Over 1000 steady RANS simulations resulted in +4.14 % CL,max (15°) and +4.56 % CL,max (30°) compared to baseline.

Supervised by Prof. Dr.-Ing. Ingo Staack and Dr.-Ing. Stanislav Karpuk
Institute of Aircraft Design and Lightweight Structures (IFL) ⋅ SE²A Cluster of Excellence

Role

  • PyAeroSweep automation
  • Mesh & parameter studies
  • OAT optimization
  • Flow analysis

Tools

  • Python ⋅ PyAeroSweep
  • Fidelity Pointwise
  • SU2 (RANS k-ω SST)
  • ParaView ⋅ Excel

Motivation

High-lift devices are critical for safe takeoff and landing. This work contributes to sustainable aviation by improving high-lift performance on next-generation HLFC wings (EU Flightpath 2050 goals).
