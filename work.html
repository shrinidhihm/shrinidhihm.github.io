Work

I am currently a Research Assistant at the Institute of Aircraft Design and Lightweight Structures (IFL), TU Braunschweig, contributing to the development of UNICADO – a Python-based university conceptual aircraft design and optimisation framework – and extending WuFoil, an open-source tool that combines Gmsh, SU2, and machine-learning-driven airfoil optimisation.

My recent Studienarbeit (2025) focused on the aerodynamic optimisation of a Fowler flap for a hybrid laminar flow control (HLFC) airfoil within the SE²A Cluster of Excellence, achieving up to +4.56 % CL,max improvement using the in-house PyAeroSweep framework.

The projects below showcase my work in aerodynamics, CFD automation, high-lift systems, conceptual aircraft design, and multidisciplinary optimisation.
